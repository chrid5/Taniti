Throughout the island our restaurants serve mainly seafood and rice within meals and some other restaurants have adopted American styled meals and Pan-Asian cuisine. The choices are vast for our tourists!
On island Taniti, our grocery stores and supermarkets are plentiful for both island habitants and tourists to be able to purchase fresh produce to enjoy a healthy meal.

Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Groceries

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.
